AI & Education

Responsible AI for learning

Artificial intelligence is becoming part of educational life, but its value depends on how critically and responsibly it is understood and used. My interest in AI in education focuses on helping teachers and schools approach these tools with clarity, caution and pedagogical purpose.

Approach

My approach to AI in education

Pedagogy first

AI should support learning goals, not replace teaching decisions.

Critical understanding

Teachers and students need to understand limitations, bias, hallucinations and data risks.

Responsible use

Privacy, transparency, copyright and ethical use must be part of school practice.

Classroom value

AI can support planning, feedback, differentiation, language practice and creative tasks when used carefully.

Important position

Teachers remain central

I do not see AI as a replacement for teachers. I see it as a tool that requires professional judgement, ethical awareness and strong pedagogical decisions.
