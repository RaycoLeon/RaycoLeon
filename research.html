Research

Research profile

My research interests sit at the intersection of language education, teacher development, educational technology and AI literacy. I am particularly interested in how schools and teachers can make informed, ethical and pedagogically sound decisions when working with digital and AI-based tools.

Current research focus

AI literacy among secondary teachers

My current work focuses on AI literacy among secondary teachers. I am interested in identifying what teachers know about AI, how confident they feel using it, what misconceptions may exist, and what kinds of training or support can help them use AI responsibly and effectively.
