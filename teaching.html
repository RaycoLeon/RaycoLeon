Teaching

Teaching profile

My teaching work focuses on helping students use English meaningfully, confidently and accurately. I aim to design lessons that combine structure, creativity, communication and reflection.

Contexts

Educational stages and contexts

Early Years and Primary

Language learning through routines, stories, play, interaction and carefully scaffolded input.

Secondary Education

English learning connected to communication, exam needs, projects and student interests.

Adult learners

Practical English learning with clear goals, useful feedback and confidence-building tasks.

International contexts

Experience with English as an additional language, bilingual education, UK and Spanish contexts.

Principles

Classroom principles

Purposeful learning

Learning should be clear, purposeful and connected to real classroom needs.

Support and challenge

Students need language support, meaningful challenge and opportunities to communicate.

Pedagogy first

Technology should serve pedagogy, not distract from learning aims.

Assessment for learning

Assessment should guide learning and help students understand their next steps.
